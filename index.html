Our Story

Welcome to Weekend Kitchen, where passion meets tradition! We're a couple of software engineers who decided to bring the authentic flavors of Rayalaseema to your doorstep during weekends and holidays.

Our journey began with a simple idea: while you relax and enjoy your precious weekend time, we'll work hard to deliver the most delicious, authentic Uggani and Bajji right to your home.

Every dish is prepared with love, using traditional recipes passed down through generations, ensuring you get that genuine taste of home-cooked food. We use cold-pressed oils in all our cooking for the healthiest, most flavorful experience.
